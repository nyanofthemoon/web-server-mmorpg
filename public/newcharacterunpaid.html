NEW CHARACTER - IMPORTANT
.....You will be starting your journey in a few moments. When you will have completed the character creation process, you will start playing Meryakon right away. This paragraph is meant to inform you about the differences between registered characters (when you have paid to play MO) and unregistered characters.

     UNREGISTERED CHARACTERS EXPIRATION
Unregistered characters are deleted once you register your account or if you have not used your account for 30 days.

     UNREGISTERED ACCOUNT GAMEPLAY LIMITATIONS:
- Titles restricted to non-adventurer titles (Outsider, Peasant and Citizen)
- Playable races: Human, Elf and Dwarf
- Maximum money: 500 cc.
- No Trial: Base Percentages Stats are set to 50%
- Cannot join nor form an alliance
- Quest restrictions
- Unable to play some disciples (Champion Disciplines and Sorcerers)
- Unable to learn most spells and abilities
- Cannot use/equip magical items or relics
- Cannot give to other players/receive from other players
- Cannot hide/recover/search for loots
- Max followers, mercenaries and beasts set to 0
- Unregistered accounts can hold up to 5 unregistered characters
- "Unregistered Character" banner in Character's History
- "Register your Account" banner during play

     WHY SHOULD I REGISTER?
- Unlock all features and become a true Adventurer/Sorcerer!
- Prices are extremely low!
- Play up to 25 characters per account!
